Meet the Founders

Adriatic Intelligence Group was founded by two visionary leaders who bring decades of combined experience in technology, AI, and strategic business transformation.

Marija Radulovic-Nastic

Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Marija is the Chief Technology Officer for Creative & Development at Electronic Arts (EA), where she leads technology strategy and innovation for one of the world's largest gaming companies. With over 20 years of experience at the intersection of technology and creativity, she has been instrumental in shaping the future of interactive entertainment.

Born and raised in Belgrade, Marija holds an MSc in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from the University of Belgrade. She has been recognized as one of the smartest minds in the gaming industry, known for her fearless leadership and ability to drive transformative change.

At EA, Marija oversees more than 1,000 game developers and is committed to leveraging AI as a copilot to amplify human creativity. She champions diversity in tech, working to create inclusive gaming communities and welcoming workplaces. Her strategic vision combines deep technical expertise with a passion for pushing boundaries and exceeding expectations.
